Happiness Choice
Which country has the highest happiness index in the world? What are the most significant factors associated with happiness? Is it linked to economic prosperity, safety, or health? This visualization will provide answers to these questions.
Global Happiness Index Map


Deeper colors represent higher happiness scores, while lighter colors indicate lower happiness scores.

Most countries score above 5. Countries in the African region tend to have lower scores.

Happiness Score Top 10



As shown in the chart, Finland is the country with the highest happiness index in the world.

The top ten countries in the ranking all have happiness scores above 7, and the majority of them are Western European nations.

Quality of Life Indicators




Israel has a healthy life expectancy score similar to other countries, but ranks lowest among the top ten in the other three indicators.


Finland has the highest scores for choice freedom and corruption perceptions, while the Netherlands has the highest score for generosity.

Social and Economic Indicators

GDP and social support scores are high in Western Europe, with Western European countries leading in both indicators. You can click on the legend to view the country distribution in different regions.
